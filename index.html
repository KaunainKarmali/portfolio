A little about me

I'm a Chartered Accountant turned Front-end Developer based in Toronto, Canada. As a Developer, I enjoy creating purposeful, accessible, and beautiful apps that place the user at the heart of development decisions.

I've spent over 5 years in client service roles, leading teams to help clients tackle their most challenging problems. These range from researching technical accounting guidance to re-imagining how a department's systems, processes, and organizational structure functions.

In my free time, I am an avid Raptors fan, enjoy reading, and staying active!
